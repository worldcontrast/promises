# World Contrast — Manifesto
### Founding Document · Version 2.0 · April 2026

*"We are not the truth. We are the record."*

## Why We Exist

Democracy produces an irreducible tension: candidates must make promises to win power, and citizens must evaluate those promises to assign power. Yet the infrastructure for this evaluation has historically been controlled by actors with their own interests — media, parties, pollsters, governments.

World Contrast was founded on a single premise:

> **The comparison of political commitments is too important to be left to anyone with an opinion.**

We are not journalists. We are not academics. We are not activists. We are archivists — operating a permanent, cryptographically sealed public ledger of what candidates promised, verbatim, to the citizens who will hold them accountable.

---

## What We Do — and What We Refuse to Do

### What we do

- **Collect** campaign promises exclusively from official primary sources
- **Authenticate** each record with a SHA-256 cryptographic hash and a permanent Wayback Machine archive
- **Classify** each promise into one of 9 standard categories — applied identically to every candidate in every country
- **Translate** each record into the 6 official UN languages
- **Publish** everything side by side, with source, date, and audit trail

### What we permanently refuse to do

- Contact candidates, parties, or campaign staff — ever
- Accept funding, sponsorship, or data contributions from political actors
- Apply human editorial judgment to the inclusion or exclusion of any statement
- Rank, score, or evaluate the quality of promises
- Publish opinion, analysis, or commentary on any record in our database
- Remove or alter any record after it has been authenticated

If a candidate says something that appears absurd, dangerous, or dishonest — we record it with the same institutional solemnity we record everything else. The record is not ours to judge.

## The Independence Certification

World Contrast certifies, publicly and permanently, that:

1. **No political actor** has ever funded, directed, or influenced our operations
2. **No government** has ever issued a legal order to modify or suppress any record
3. **No candidate or party** has ever been given advance notice of what we intend to publish
4. **No advertiser** has ever paid to appear in our platform
5. **No employee or contributor** holds a position in any political party, campaign, or government electoral body

This certification is updated with every deployment. Any change to this status will be announced publicly in this repository before it takes effect.

*If this certification is ever violated, the violation will itself be published here.*

## The Eight Pillars

### I — Strict Editorial Neutrality

We do not opine, interpret, or judge. The registry is the only artifact. The citizen is the judge.

### II — Technological Equivalence

Processing limits are identical for every political actor in the same election. Algorithmic symmetry is not a feature — it is a constitutional requirement of this project.

### III — Permanent Public Audit

Every statement extracted is cryptographically sealed into the permanent record. Every statement rejected is logged publicly with the exact reason for rejection. Nothing is silently discarded.

### IV — Right to Compare Directly

Viewing the commitments of competing candidates side by side is a fundamental civic right. No registration. No paywall. No friction. No mediation.

### V — Documentary Traceability

No record in our database exists without its exact source URL, collection timestamp, SHA-256 content hash, and Wayback Machine archive link. Provenance is not optional — it is the product.

### VI — Political Independence

Zero communication with campaigns. Zero funding from political actors. Zero government direction. We read only what candidates publish publicly for the world to see.

### VII — Open Infrastructure

The code, the extraction methodology, the POCVA-01 protocol, and the database schema are fully open source (AGPL v3.0). Anyone can audit the algorithm. Anyone can verify that no bias is encoded in the system.

### VIII — Zero Commercial Interference

No financial transaction can distort search results, purchase placement, or sponsor metrics in this registry. Enterprise revenue from our Live API subsidizes free public access — it never influences what we collect or how we display it.

## The POCVA-01 Protocol

Every extraction decision made by our AI agent is governed by the **Autonomous Collection and Validation Operational Protocol — version 01**.

The core rule is the **Promise Equation**:

```
[P] = [A] Actor + [V] Future Action Verb + [M] Measurable Target
```

A statement that does not satisfy all three components is not a promise — it is rhetoric, and it is rejected and logged. The algorithm does not negotiate. The algorithm does not have opinions. The algorithm is the answer.

The full protocol is published at `agents/extraction/prompts/extraction_prompt.txt`. Its SHA-256 hash is recorded alongside every promise it produces, providing irrefutable proof of which version of the protocol was active at the time of extraction.

## On Truth

We are frequently asked: *"But what if a candidate lies?"*

We do not answer that question. It is not our question to answer.

Our function is to record what was promised — faithfully, permanently, and with full provenance. The question of whether a promise was kept, broken, or honest belongs to journalists, courts, historians, and above all, to citizens.

World Contrast is not the truth. World Contrast is the record.

The record is yours.
